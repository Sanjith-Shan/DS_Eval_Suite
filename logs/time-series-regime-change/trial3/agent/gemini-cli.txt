1.  **Regime Change Detection**: By analyzing the `sales.csv` data, I identified a significant and immediate "regime change" (renovation) occurring on **June 26, 2023**. At this point, sales approximately doubled from a baseline of ~92 to ~190.
2.  **Seasonality Analysis**:
    *   **Weekly**: Sales exhibit a strong weekly cycle where Saturday and Sunday are significantly lower (approx. 60% of weekday levels).
    *   **Monthly/Yearly**: There is a notable seasonal spike in November and December, with sales being approximately 35-40% higher than the January baseline.
3.  **Model Selection**: I employed a **Log-Linear Regression** model. This approach was chosen because the seasonality and renovation effects were found to be multiplicative (i.e., the amplitude of seasonal swings scaled with the baseline level). The model includes:
    *   A linear trend component.
    *   A binary indicator for the post-renovation period.
    *   One-hot encoded seasonal dummies for the day of the week and month.
4.  **Forecasting**: The model was trained on the log-transformed sales data and used to predict the next 30 days (starting 2024-12-31). The predictions were then transformed back to the original scale using the exponential function.
