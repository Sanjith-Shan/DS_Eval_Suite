### Statistical Findings
- **Marginal Correlation:** The raw correlation between `ice_cream_sales` and `drowning_deaths` is high (~0.76), which initially suggests a strong relationship.
- **Confounder Identification:** `temperature_f` was identified as a major confounding variable. It is highly correlated with both ice cream sales (~0.93) and drowning deaths (~0.79).

### Recommendation
The proposed ban on ice cream is not supported by the data, as ice cream consumption is not a causal factor in drowning deaths. Both variables are instead driven by higher temperatures during the summer months.
