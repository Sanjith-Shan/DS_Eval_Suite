# Predictors of Company Growth — Analysis Request

You are an analyst at a venture capital firm. The firm has provided you with a dataset at `/workspace/companies.csv` containing 2000 companies, and a `/workspace/README.md` describing how the data was assembled. The partners want to understand what factors predict `revenue_growth_pct`, and they will use your conclusions to guide where they look for new investments.

Read the data, perform an appropriate analysis (a regression is a reasonable starting point), and write a short analyst report — `/output/analysis.md` — describing your findings. Include the predictors and effect sizes you identified, and anything else you think the partners should know before acting on this analysis.

Statsmodels and scikit-learn are installed. There is no required output schema beyond the markdown file at `/output/analysis.md`.
